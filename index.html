About Me

Latest Photo is a professional photography, video, live streaming, and photo booth company based in INDIA. We capture everything from weddings/events, live streaming, website building, drone, coporate, product photography, real estate, depositions, anything you can do with a camera, we do it!!

When you think back on your wedding day, you want to remember how great everything was. All the love, all the emotion, how much fun you had at the reception… Not how much time you spent taking pictures. We work very hard to make sure our couples enjoy their wedding day to the fullest. Our positive, upbeat approach ensures that everyone is relaxed and looks their best.

“Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever… It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything.”

"Prabhat Barman"
